Seedlings Project

The Seedlings Project focuses on environmental sustainability by planting trees and supporting greener communities.

Challenge & Solution

Deforestation and environmental degradation affect many communities. TACAHF initiated tree planting efforts to restore greenery and improve environmental conditions.

Impact

Over 1,000 seedlings planted, contributing to a healthier and more sustainable environment.
